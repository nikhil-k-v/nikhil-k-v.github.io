CYDLOIDAL ACTUATOR

Gearboxes, transmissions, and actuators are extremely interesting to me, and for my current main project - creating a 6 degree of freedom robotic arm with a friend (Sahil) - we needed reducers to increase our NEMA stepper motorsâ€™ torque output and precision. Rather than buy/find actuators online, I chose to generate them myself to learn function driven modeling and apply some of my knowledge in parametric functions & cycloids. Using the Desmos API & Fusion 360s Equation Driven Curve add-in, I created a parametric cycloidal gear generator which can test the parameters and animate visualize/animate the cycloid you want. The generator was successfully used in creating the actuators for the robotic arm, and you can try it out HERE.
